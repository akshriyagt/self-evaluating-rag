Company Handbook: Acme Robotics

Section 1: Leave Policy
Employees at Acme Robotics are entitled to 18 days of paid annual leave per
calendar year. Leave requests must be submitted at least 5 working days in
advance through the internal HR portal. Unused leave can be carried over to
the next year up to a maximum of 5 days. Sick leave is separate from annual
leave and covers up to 10 days per year without requiring a medical
certificate for absences under 3 days.

Section 2: Remote Work Policy
Acme Robotics operates on a hybrid model. Employees are expected to work
from the office at least 3 days per week, with Tuesday and Thursday being
mandatory in-office days for all engineering teams. Fully remote work
arrangements require approval from both the manager and HR, and are
generally granted only for roles that do not require lab or hardware access.

Section 3: Expense Reimbursement
Employees can claim reimbursement for business-related expenses including
travel, client meals, and approved software subscriptions. All claims must
be submitted within 30 days of the expense with itemized receipts. The
approval limit for a direct manager is 15,000 INR; anything above that
requires finance team sign-off.

Section 4: Performance Reviews
Performance reviews are conducted twice a year, in April and October.
Reviews include a self-assessment, manager assessment, and peer feedback
from at least two colleagues. Ratings range from "Needs Improvement" to
"Outstanding" and directly influence the annual bonus calculation.

Section 5: Equipment Policy
New employees receive a laptop, monitor, and ergonomic chair within their
first week. Equipment requests for additional peripherals (keyboards,
headsets, docking stations) can be made through the IT portal and are
typically fulfilled within 5 business days.
